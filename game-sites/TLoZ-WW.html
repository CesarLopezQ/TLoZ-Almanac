The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker

The tenth game in the franchise, originally released for the Game Cube, and later remastered to the Wii U.

The game was the first in the series to use cell-shading, leading to the cartoon like appareance.

The gameplay is similar to early 3D Zelda games such as Ocarina of Time and Majoras Mask, with the key point being a pseudo open world experience, traveling a vast ocean map filled with islands.
